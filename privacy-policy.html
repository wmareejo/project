Privacy Policy

  • Device & Usage Info: We may collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage patterns (e.g., what topics you view).

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • With your consent

4. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your login or quiz preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Contact us regarding any privacy concerns

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or loss.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of any major changes by posting an update on this page.

8. Contact Us
