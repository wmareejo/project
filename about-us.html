About Us

CodeQuest is a student-driven educational platform designed to make technical learning easy and engaging for everyone.

Our Mission

We aim to provide clear, beginner-friendly explanations of programming, databases, and computer networks — all in one place. Whether you're a student or just curious, CodeQuest is here to help you grow your knowledge.

The Team

This project is developed by a group of eight dedicated students working together to design, develop, and launch a full beginner-friendly educational website. We’re proud to bring you:

  • Programming Tutorials
  • Networking Concepts
  • Database Knowledge
  • Fun, interactive quizzes

Why We Created CodeQuest

As students ourselves, we know that learning technical topics can feel overwhelming. We created CodeQuest to simplify things and gather some of the most important concepts all in one place.

Our resources

For our resources, the information we gathered in each topic was taken from our course materials in KKU, OpenAI, and GeeksforGeeks.
